## Privacy Policy  
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.  

### What We Collect  
- Login credentials through secure Google-based sign-in.  
- User-provided name, avatar (profile photo), and gender.  
- Basic usage analytics (e.g., session frequency, interactions).  

We do **not** collect device information, contact lists, or precise location data.  

### How We Use Your Data  
- To verify your identity and enable account access.  
- To support AI-driven conversations and user matching.  
- To analyze usage patterns and improve app quality.  

### Third-Party Services  
Trusted providers assist with:  
- Account authentication.  
- AI conversations.  
- **Content moderation and safety** (see [Content Moderation](#content-moderation--safety)).  

Partners receive only minimal necessary data.  

### Data Retention & Deletion  
Data is retained only as long as needed. Request deletion via [Contact](#contact).

## Content Moderation & Safety  
To ensure a safe community, we implement:  

### 1. User Agreements  
- All users must accept our **End-User License Agreement (EULA)** during sign-up, which explicitly prohibits:  
  - Objectionable content (e.g., hate speech, bullying).  
  - Abusive behavior (e.g., harassment, threats).  

### 2. Content Filtering  
- **Automated filters** block offensive language and inappropriate material.  
- **Human review** for reported content.  

### 3. Reporting & Blocking  
- **Flagging**: Users can report objectionable content via in-app buttons.  
- **Blocking**: Users can block abusive accounts instantly.  

### 4. Moderation Actions  
- **24-hour response**: Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.  
- **Enforcement**: Offending content is removed; repeat offenders are banned.
